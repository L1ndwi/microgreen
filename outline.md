## Page Sections

### Index.html
1. **Navigation Bar**
   - Logo/Brand name
   - Navigation links (Features, About, Contact)
   - Mobile hamburger menu

2. **Hero Section**
   - Animated background with particles
   - Main headline with typewriter effect
   - Subheadline about product benefits
   - Hero image of growing microgreens
   - Primary CTA button (Join Waitlist)

3. **Product Overview**
   - 3 feature cards with icons
   - Hydroponic System
   - Smart LED Lighting
   - Effortless Cleaning

4. **How It Works**
   - 3-step process with illustrations
   - Step 1: Add seeds and water
   - Step 2: LED light does the work
   - Step 3: Harvest in 7-12 days

5. **Email Signup Section**
   - Waitlist signup form
   - Name and email inputs
   - Success message handling

6. **Tech Specs** (Expandable)
   - Product specifications table
   - Certifications and materials

7. **Footer**
   - Copyright information
   - Social media links
   - Privacy policy link

### Features.html
- Detailed feature explanations
- Comparison with traditional growing
- Benefits and advantages

### About.html
- Company story and mission
- Sustainability focus
- Team information

### Contact.html
- Contact form
- Support information
- FAQ section

## Technical Implementation

- **CSS Framework**: Tailwind CSS
- **Animations**: Anime.js, Typed.js
- **Forms**: Formspree integration
- **Images**: Optimized loading
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first design
- **Performance**: Lazy loading, optimized assets
